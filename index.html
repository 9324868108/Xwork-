Commission Structure

Xwork offers a revolutionary commission structure designed to support freelancers:

  • First 6 Months: All new freelancers enjoy a 0% commission rate for the first six months after joining the platform. This means you keep 100% of your earnings during this period.
  • After 6 Months: Following the initial six-month period, a flat 5% commission will be applied to all earnings. This rate is guaranteed and will not increase.
  • Subscription Services: Freelancers can optionally subscribe to visibility-boosting services that enhance profile placement in search results. These subscriptions are separate from the commission structure.
  • Payment Processing: Standard payment processing fees may apply depending on your chosen payment method. These fees are separate from Xwork's commission.

By using Xwork, you agree to these terms and acknowledge that the commission structure may be subject to change with appropriate notice for new users. Existing users will always be grandfathered into their original terms.
